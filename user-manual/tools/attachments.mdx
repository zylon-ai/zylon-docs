title: Chat attachments

Attach image files directly in your chat messages. When a file is attached, Zylon can analyze its content, and use it as context when responding.

<Warning>
    Ask your IT administrator to enable the Multimodal capabilities in your system configuration [with the Operator Manual](/operator-manual/configuration/features).
</Warning>

---

## How to attach a file

1. In the chat input, click the **attachment (paperclip) icon**. You can also drag & drop it.
2. Select an image from your device
3. Add your message(It is limited to 1 file per message)
4. Send your message

The attachment button is located **to the left of the source selector menu**.

---

## Limitations

- Image understanding depends on clarity and resolution
- Very large or complex images may produce less detailed results
- Your model has to support image capabilities
- Only one image is allowed per chat request
