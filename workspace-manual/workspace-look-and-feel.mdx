As an administrator, you can customize Zylon Workspace to match your organization's branding and preferences. Zylon is a fully white-label solution.

*[Screenshot placeholder: Admin settings interface showing branding customization options]*

## Customization Options

**Company Branding**
- **Workspace Name**: Choose the name of the Workspace (e.g., "Acme GPT")
- **Company Logo**: Upload your organization's logo for the interface
- **AI Assistant Name**: Choose what to call your AI (e.g., "Acme Assistant", "CompanyBot")

*[Screenshot placeholder: Branding preview showing customized interface with company logo]*

## Accessing Customization Settings

**Administrator Access Required**

1. **Click on the Workspace name** on the top left
2. **Select "Settings"** section
3. **Make your changes** to the Workspace Name and Logo
4. **Save and apply** changes organization-wide

<Warning>
Only users with Administrator privileges can modify workspace appearance. Contact your IT team if you need these permissions.
</Warning>

## White-Label Configuration

**Complete Brand Control**
- Remove all Zylon branding if desired
- Use your company name throughout the interface
- Custom login screens and welcome messages

**Professional Appearance**
- Maintain consistent corporate identity
- Ensure compliance with brand guidelines
- Create familiar experience for users
