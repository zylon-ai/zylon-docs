Attach image an audio files directly in your chat messages. When a file is attached, Zylon can analyze its content, and use it as context when responding.

---

## How to attach a file

1. In the chat input, click the **attachment (paperclip) icon**. You can also drag & drop it.
2. Select an image or audio from your device
3. Add your message (optional but recommended)
4. Send the message

The attachment button is located **to the left of the source selector menu**.

---

## Limitations

- Image understanding depends on clarity and resolution
- Very large or complex images may produce less detailed results
- Your model has to support image and/or audio capabilities
- Feature has to be enabled on
